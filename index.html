Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Smart Citizen, Smart Government, Smart Society, and Smart Economy.

Sustainability

The three pillars of sustainability in Bangladesh are environmental, social, and economic

Infrastructure

infrastructure include Smart Bangladesh, Digital Bangladesh, and climate change mitigation.

Empowerment

Empowerment is a process that allows people to experience personal and social change.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 represents a forward-looking vision aimed at transforming the nation into a hub of technological innovation and digital efficiency. This initiative seeks to integrate advanced technologies such as artificial intelligence, blockchain, and the Internet of Things (IoT) into the fabric of everyday life.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Bangladesh's vision for a green energy future includes increasing the use of renewable energy sources and reducing the use of fuels.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
